What Makes a Squishmallow Rare?

Rarity Factors:

  • Limited Edition Releases - Small production runs
  • Store Exclusives - Only available at specific retailers
  • Convention Exclusives - Comic-Con, SDCC, etc.
  • Discontinued Designs - No longer in production
  • Early Releases (2017-2019) - Original collection harder to find
  • Regional Exclusives - Only sold in certain countries
  • Collab Editions - Brand partnerships (Disney, Sanrio, etc.)
