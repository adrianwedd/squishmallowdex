### Google Search Console (10 minutes)

**Step 1: Add Your Property**
1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click **"Add Property"**
3. Enter: `https://squishmallowdex.com`
4. Choose **"URL prefix"** method

**Step 2: Verify Ownership**
Choose one method:
- **HTML tag** (easiest): Copy the meta tag and add it to your site
- **HTML file**: Download file and upload to your site
- **DNS record**: Add TXT record to your DNS (recommended if you have access)
- **Google Analytics**: If you already have GA connected (you do!)

**Step 3: Submit Sitemap**
1. Click **"Sitemaps"** in left sidebar
2. Enter: `sitemap.xml`
3. Click **"Submit"**

**Expected Results:**
- Initial processing: 1-2 hours
- Full indexing: 1-2 weeks
- Check status in "Coverage" report

---

### Bing Webmaster Tools (5 minutes)

**Step 1: Add Your Site**
1. Go to: https://www.bing.com/webmasters
2. Click **"Add a site"**
3. Enter: `https://squishmallowdex.com`

**Step 2: Verify Ownership**
Choose one method:
- **XML file verification** (easiest)
- **Meta tag**
- **CNAME record**

**Step 3: Submit Sitemap**
1. Click **"Sitemaps"** in left menu
2. Click **"Submit sitemap"**
3. Enter: `https://squishmallowdex.com/sitemap.xml`
4. Click **"Submit"**

**Pro Tip:** If you verify with Google Search Console, you can import to Bing automatically!

---

### Yandex Webmaster (Optional - 5 minutes)

For Russian/Eastern European traffic:
1. Go to: https://webmaster.yandex.com
2. Add site: `https://squishmallowdex.com`
3. Submit sitemap: `https://squishmallowdex.com/sitemap.xml`
